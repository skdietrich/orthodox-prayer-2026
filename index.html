iPhone install notes

How to use this on iPhone

  1. For simple use, open index.html in Safari or another browser.
  2. For full PWA behavior, place the folder on any HTTPS static host, open it in Safari, tap Share, then Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Sync May 21 to Dec 31 while online to cache daily calendar data for offline use.
